FULL DESCRIPTION:
TruthLens is a free Chrome extension that protects you from AI 
misinformation in real time.

Every AI — ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, Copilot — confidently says things 
that are completely wrong. Doctors get wrong drug dosages. Students get 
fake citations. Lawyers get invented case law. Nobody knows when AI is 
lying until it is too late.

TruthLens sits on top of any AI tool and shows a coloured dot next to 
every sentence:

🟢 GREEN — Verified by Wikipedia, PubMed, or trusted databases
🟡 YELLOW — Could not verify — treat with caution  
🔴 RED — Contradicts known sources — do not act on this

HOW IT WORKS:
TruthLens reads every sentence the AI writes. For each factual claim, 
it cross-checks against Wikipedia, PubMed (medical research), and 
Wikidata in real time. Results appear as coloured dots in 2-3 seconds 
— automatically, without you doing anything.

WHO IT HELPS:
- Students — catch fake citations before submitting assignments
- Doctors and nurses — verify medical information from AI
- Journalists — fact-check AI-assisted content before publishing
- Lawyers — catch invented case references
- Everyone — know when to trust AI and when to verify

COMPLETELY FREE:
No subscription. No account. No data sold. Open source.

PRIVACY:
Only the sentence text is sent for verification. No personal data, 
no browsing history, no login information is ever collected.

TECH: Built with TypeScript, Chrome Manifest V3, Groq Llama 3.3, 
Wikipedia API, PubMed NCBI API, PostgreSQL caching.
